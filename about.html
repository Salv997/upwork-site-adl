Antilla Di Lauro

Born and raised in Milan, Antilla is an architect and designer. After obtaining a Master’s degree with honors in Architecture from Politecnico di Milano, she started her career at Patricia Urquiola Studio in Milan working on upscale retail and hospitality projects. Driven by a deep-rooted passion for travelling and for luxury design, she then relocated to Dubai to work for HBA/Hirsch Bedner Associates, the world’s leading hospitality interior design firm. Antilla has designed some of the world’s most prestigious hotels for operators such as Four Seasons, Langham, St. Regis and she was awarded HBA Dubai Designer of The Year in 2018. Fascinated by learning from new challenges and crossing design and cultural boundaries she then moved to Saudi Arabia to work for the Crown Prince Private Affairs where she managed the design of His Royal Highness private palaces. Building on these remarkable experiences, Antilla decided to start developing her own designs and focusing on creating intriguing and refined rugs for today’s lifestyle. Her experimental approach celebrates the juxtaposition between sophistication and comfort, multi-layered and contemporary, poetry and geometry, avoiding style constraints and thinking out of the box. Nourished by tireless attention to detail and engaging color palettes, vibrant yet welcoming, her unconventional creations not only capture the eye but foster an emotive connection.
